About Me - Conor Rom

My name is Conor Rom. I was born in Rock Hill, South Carolina. I am the oldest child of two amazing parents, a retired Army Officer and an Antique Shop Owner. I spent much of my childhood as a military brat living in Kansas, Colorado, even England, before moving back to South Carolina. I am currently a student at The Citadel, the Military College of South Carolina, studying Accounting. I have a deep love of coding and am self-taught in website and video game coding. I am currently working as a season Bank Teller at First Palmetto Bank (Not Sponsored). I love 3D printing, investing, stratergy games especially RTSs, coding, miniature wargaming, Warhammer 40K, and South Carolina History (especially American Revolution).
