Custom 3D Printing

RomCon Industries prides itself on our 3D printing technology and skills. If you would like something 3D printed, and already have a 3D file, please download the "3D Print Quote Form".

Then email the quote form along with the STL or GCODE file, if you have the files, to Conor.X.Rom@gmail.com with the label Custom 3D Print - "Your Name". Check out the example email below. If you don't have the STL or GCODE file, don't worry, as long as the link to the website holding the file is filled out on the quote form. Also check out our Custom 3D Design page, if you cannot find what you want printed and we could possible design it.

We will reply to your email sometime in the next 24 hours, with a quote from us, or further questions.

What Is Copyright?

Copyright is a type of intellectual property that protects original works; allowing the owner the exclusive right to copy, distribute, adapt, display, and/or perform a creative work. Currently, these protections last the life of the creator plus 70 years.

RomCon Industries is only able to 3D print files that the author has given their Copyright somewhat to the public, or given us a private license to use them. Any 3D file licensed under Non-Commercial Creative Commons or is dedicated to the Public Domain we can print. If you cannot find exactly what you want, check out our Custom 3D Design page.
